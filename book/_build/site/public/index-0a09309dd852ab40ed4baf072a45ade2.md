# What is HyPyp?

"Hyperscanning" setups are used to simultaneously record brain activity from two or more individuals during social tasks and to investigate the co-variations in their brain activity related to their socio-behavioral interactions. The Hyperscanning Python Pipeline (HyPyP) is a open-source software package that allows to carry-out and interpret a wide range of inter-brain connectivity analyses.
